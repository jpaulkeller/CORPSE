Athletic Alex
Alex gets +1 to Move for each empty Equipment slot (not counting extra slots).
Birder Brandon
Brandon has an eye for details; he gets +1 when searching for Multi-caches.
Collector Colin
Colin can carry any number of Travel Bugs; and he starts the game with a random one.
Determined Dan
Once per turn, Dan may re-roll a .D. when searching.
Eager Earl
When Earl gets equipment from a cache, he may draw an extra card to choose from.
Fast Freddie
Whenever a new cache is placed on the board, Freddie may immediately move 4 tiles.
Grampa Gary
Gary takes his time; he gets -1 to all Move rolls, and +1 to all Search rolls.
Hunter Henry
Henry gets +1 when searching in Forest tiles. Other players can't play events on Henry if he's on a Forest tile.
Independent Isabel
Other players may only play events on Isabel if they are in the same map quadrant.






Jolly Jamie
Other players can't play events on Jamie unless she has more points than they do.
Kindly Kris
Kris can discard Event cards to gain 1 point each.
Lucky Lisa
Lisa gets +1 whenever she rolls doubles.
Marathon Mike
Tiles next to a path count as a path for Mike.
Nosey Norman
Whenever another player rolls 5 or higher, Norman may immediately move 1 tile.
Observant Oscar
If another player is within 2 tiles of him, Oscar gets +2 on his roll.
Puzzler Paul
Paul gets +1 when searching for puzzle caches.
Quirky Quigly
Quigly may hold up to 3 Event cards; and he starts the game with a random one.
Ranger Rachel
Rachel gets +2 to all Move rolls in Forest tiles.






Scout Scotty
Scotty may discard Equipment cards to gain 1 point each.
Tireless Ted
Ted may treat any "end your turn" effect as -2 instead.
Unique Ursula
Whenever Ursula rolls a 1 on either die, she gets +1 on her roll.
Volunteer Veda
Veda gains 1 extra point for Event cards that award her points. She must attend all Meet and Greet events.
Wandering Warren
Warren doesn't get the +1 path bonus, but if he rolls a .F. while moving, he may take an extra turn.
Yuppie Yuri
When Yuri finds a cache, he may keep both Equipment cards if he discards one of his.
